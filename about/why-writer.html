Why Writer? Meet Jack

I’m Jack, an MBA with a healthy skepticism for traditional approaches to business. I've dedicated my career to solving complex challenges in learning, marketing, and technology.

Professional Journey

At Chegg Skills (NYSE: CHGG), I spearheaded marketing and growth initiatives, transforming a coding bootcamp into an enterprise upskilling solution. This project turned into the company’s fastest-growing business line, reshaping how we approach professional development.

Previously, I was the inaugural marketing hire at Panorama Education (YC '13), where I developed and led demand generation and product marketing strategies. My efforts helped scale the company to support over 5 million users, significantly broadening our impact.

What I Bring to Writer

  • Go-to-Market Leadership: I've built and managed a team of over six direct reports, launching AI skills programs and generative AI lead generation tools.
  • Solutions Positioning: I have crafted customized messaging and positioning for diverse industries including insurance, healthcare, and financial services.
  • AI Writing Pioneer: I led the deployment of Jasper.ai, focusing on training and enablement, which significantly sped up our time to market for new blogs, ads, emails, and customer case studies.
  • Strategic Thinking: I adeptly shifted our go-to-market messaging from product-centric features to solutions-focused outcomes, enhancing pipeline growth and product differentiation.

Personal Snapshot

When I'm not working, I'm likely hitting the trails for a run, skiing down slopes, or hunting down tickets to the next big concert. I'm a proud Jumbo and Wahoo, and a devoted Patriots fan, forever cherishing their golden era. These days, I'm living and working in Nashville with my fiancé and our dog, Mowgli.
