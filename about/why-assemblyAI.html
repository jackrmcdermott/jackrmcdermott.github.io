Why AssemblyAI?

I’m Jack, an MBA with a healthy skepticism for traditional business. It's why I've committed my career to solving complex business challenges through marketing and technology.

Speech holds special meaning for me. Having successfully lived with a speech impediment for my whole life, I recognize the power and potential to unlock voice for the next generation of technology products and services.

Professional Journey

At Chegg Skills (NYSE: CHGG), I led product marketing and growth initiatives, transforming a developer bootcamp into an enterprise upskilling solution. I rebuilt our product's messaging and positioning, leading to the fastest-growing business line in the company.

Previously, I was the first marketing hire at Panorama Education (YC '13), where I built our product marketing function and scaled it through the company's growth stage. My efforts helped win new clients and reach more than 5 million students every year.

Before that, I co-founded a company while in college to develop speech apps for therapists long before models like Universal-1 existed.

What I Bring to AssemblyAI

  • Product Marketing Leadership: I've built product marketing from the ground floor to the growth stage, recruiting and managing a team of 6+ direct reports.
  • Positioning Expertise: I crafted tailored messaging and positioning for highly-competitive technical industries with a focus on sales enablement and revenue.
  • Technical Perspective: I've marketed technical programs to software developers and data scientists, and their teams and enterprise leaders for upmarket growth.
  • Strategic Thinking: I adapted go-to market strategy and resources with customer success, education, product, and sales to create best-in-class customer experiences.

Personal Snapshot

When I'm not working, I'm likely on ski trails or bike routes, or scouting for last-minute concert tickets. I'm a proud Jumbo and Wahoo, and a devoted Patriots fan forever reliving their golden era. These days, I live in Nashville with my fiancé and our dog, Mowgli.

I’m excited about the future of AssemblyAI, where I believe we can drive forward next-generation AI products and services powered by speech.
