Speech4Good (2011-2013)

While in college, I founded Speech4Good. Our mission was to help people (like me) with speech disorders access proven therapy tools from their pocket.

We built several iOS apps in the earlier days of the App Store. Our apps reached 25K+ users, and we raised seed funding and were named one of Inc. Magazine's "Coolest College Startups" in 2013.

 

Fluently (2012)

In building speech apps, I launched Fluently to visualize stuttering in real-time. My team developed advanced audio models that analyzed real-time voice trends and detected moments of disfluent speech. The app promoted improved speech through a technique called easy onset.

The app was well-received by speech therapists and users. Yet the technical work proved more difficult than imagined, so we sunsetted the app about a year later. Looking back, it's still one of my favorites.
