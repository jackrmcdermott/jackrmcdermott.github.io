Chegg Skills (Today)

Today, I lead marketing at Chegg Skills. We help companies develop talent with the right skills to get business results and unlock career mobility for their workforce.

Our focus with Fortune 500 partners is training their people with the latest technical and durable skills. Just recently, I authored our first inaugural Impact Report to demonstrate the return on investment in our upskilling programs.

At Chegg, I previously led business design and growth for early-stage product concepts for college students. This contributed to new product directions now under widescale development.

Panorama Education (2014-2019)

I began my career at Panorama Education, an early-stage edtech company backed by Y-Combinator (YC'13) and Google Ventures. I was the company's first marketing hire and led early go-to market, brand and product marketing

Panorama led the way in building better tools to support students. While there, I helped launch new products, directed documentary shorts and produced new data insights. Today, the company serves 15 million K-12 students.

Get to Know You Survey (2015)

At Panorama, I led a team of engineers and educators to build the Get to Know You Survey. 100K+ students and teachers used the survey to find their commonalities. We partnered with Harvard researchers to publish their findings on NPR's Hidden Brain.

Speech4Good (2011-2013)

I founded Speech4Good while in college. The mission was to help people (like me) with speech disorders access proven therapy tools. We built iOS apps that reached 25K+ users and raised some funding. We were named one of Inc. Magazine's "Coolest College Startups."
