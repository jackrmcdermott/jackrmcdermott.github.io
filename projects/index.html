Panorama Education (2014-2019)

I joined Panorama Education, an early-stage education tech company backed by Y-Combinator and Google Ventures. Our mission was to radically improve student outcomes in every US K-12 school.

I was the company's first marketing hire. My work enabled new product launches, customer documentaries and data insights. Today, Panorama supports schools that serve 15M+ students, or nearly one in three US K-12 students.

At Panorama, I led a team of engineers and educators to build a survey app that 100K+ students and teachers used to discover their commonalities. We partnered with Harvard researchers whose findings were later featured on NPR's Hidden Brain.

Speech4Good (2011-2013)

While in college, I founded Speech4Good. Our mission was to help people (like me) with speech disorders access proven therapy tools.

We built iOS apps that reached 25K+ users, raised a small seed funding round, and were named one of Inc. Magazine's "Coolest College Startups."

 

Fluently (2012)

I launched Fluently to visualize stuttering in real-time. My team developed advanced audio models to detect moments of disfluent speech, providing users with speech impediments visual feedback for therapy use.

The technical work proved more difficult than imagined, so our team sunsetted the app about a year later. Looking back, it's still one of my favorites.
